Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the AWS Acceleration Funding program? +

The AWS Acceleration Funding program provides eligible AWS Partner organisations with up to 70-75% funding toward instructor-led AWS training and certification. Each enrolment includes the training delivery (1, 2, or 3 day courses) plus an AWS exam voucher. The program is delivered through AWS Advanced Tier Training Partners — Bespoke Training has held this status since 2013.

Who is eligible for the Acceleration Funding? +

AWS Partner organisations at the Select, Advanced, and Premier tier levels are eligible. Partners need to maintain specific certification requirements to meet their tier obligations — the Acceleration Funding helps cover the cost of getting your team trained and certified.

What's included in each enrolment? +

Each enrolment includes the instructor-led training delivery (1, 2, or 3 day courses depending on the topic) plus an AWS certification exam voucher. AWS funds up to 70-75% of the total cost through the Acceleration Funding program.

What training areas are covered? +

The program covers five capability areas: Gen AI & Machine Learning (Amazon Bedrock, SageMaker, Prompt Engineering, Responsible AI), Cloud Infrastructure, Application Development, Cost Optimisation, and Security & Risk. All are delivered as instructor-led, hands-on courses.

What happens on the discovery call? +

It's a free, 30-minute Google Meet call with Trent (Founder, Bespoke Training). We'll understand your partnership tier, current certification status, and capability gaps — then build a tailored enablement plan that leverages the Acceleration Funding available to you.

How do I know which certifications my team needs? +

Each partnership tier (Select, Advanced, Premier) has specific requirements for accredited individuals, foundational certifications, and technical certifications. During the discovery call, we'll map your current team's certifications against your tier requirements and identify the gaps.
